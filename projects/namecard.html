Name Card

Designed in Illustrator and Photoshop.

Overview

My name card, designed in Illustrator and Photoshop, features a minimalist design with a focus on typography. The card is double-sided, with the front featuring my logo, and the back featuring my contact information.

Purpose

Originally, this was made for a class project, though as I worked more on it, I realized that I could use it as my own name card. I wanted to create a design that was simple yet elegant, and I believe that I have achieved that with this design.

Process

This project was made in Illustrator and Photoshop. I started by sketching out some ideas on paper, and then moved on to creating the design in Illustrator.

I came across some images featuring a name card that looked like an ID, and I was inspired by that design. I decided to make my name card look like a mini ID card, with my logo on the back. I didn't want just another basic name card though, so I decided to play around with the perspectives of the information to the right of the card image.

I decided to put my contact information on a wall tilting away from the user. Gettinng the perspective of the room was difficult, although I soon found a brilliant solution. I went onto Minecraft, a classic game that I had played in my childhood, and built a replica room. By using this room. I was able to get the angles for the corner of each wall just rght.

I then went on to place my text on the wall, and with the help of perspective warp and some quick touches, I was done! All in all, this project taught me more about Illustrator, and it also encouraged me to think outside the box. What other games can I use as a tool for my designs?
